GAMEBALOOZA

Instructions

  1. The team should contain maximum of 2 members
  2. The events consists of three rounds
  3. Each round is an elimination round.
  4. Winners will be awarded with cash prizes.

Description

  1. The 1st round is an elimination round, the team needs to build a pyramid with the help of cup and balloon. The team which completes their task within the given time are allowed to move for the next round.
  2. In 2nd round the players need to blow the ping pong ball from one cup to another within a timelimit top five teams are allowed for the next round.
  3. In final round the player has to guess the words or sentences and the team which guesses most words in the given time will be declared as the winner.
