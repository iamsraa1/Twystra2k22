CONNECT TECHY

Instructions

  1. Each team consist of 2 members..
  2. The game includes 3 rounds(teams would be filtered from each round).
  3. The participants should try to connect the images to find a technical term.
  4. The team which finds the answer first should raise their hands.

Description

  1. The 1st round is an elimination round, where only the top ten teams are selected based on their scores ,to the next round. 1st round consists of 20 questions.
  2. 2nd round consists of 15 questions. Only three teams are to filtered to the final round .
  3. The winner would be declared at the final round, this round consists of 10 questions.
