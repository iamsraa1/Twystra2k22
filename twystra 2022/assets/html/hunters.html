HUNTERS AND GATHERERS

Instructions

  1. The team should contain two members.
  2. The event consists of maximum of 10 sequential rounds(clues).
  3. The winner of the event would be declared based on finding the treasure at final clue or completing as many of the clues as possible.
  4. Violating the rules,property damage or any kind of cheating leads to disqualification.

Description

  1. The aim is to find the treasure at different spots from the clues before the time limit.
  2. The first of the clues will be chosen from the lot by each team.
  3. Each hint leads to an object, nearby which next clue will be hidden.
  4. At each hunt, the teams will be filtered.
  5. The teams should report at the event venue after finding each clue.
